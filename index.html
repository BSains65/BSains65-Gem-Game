Infinity Stone Collector

Here's how you play the game


  1. You are now Thanos
  2. You will be given a random number of souls to collect at the start of the game. "Number To Get"
  3. There are four infinity stones below.
  • Space
  • Time
  • Reality
  • Mind
  1. By clicking on an infinity stone, you will add a specific amount of souls to your total .
  2. You win the game by matching your total souls to the random number of souls.
  3. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each infinity stone is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each stone.
